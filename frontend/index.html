Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our custom database integrations and Power BI workspaces.

What is Nexiora?

Nexiora is a managed business intelligence and analytics service. We build secure data pipelines to connect your accounting, payment, sales, and marketing platforms, and bundle them into custom Power BI dashboards. Every client is assigned a dedicated analyst to keep metrics current and handle dashboard modifications.

Who is Nexiora for?

Nexiora is built for growing e-commerce platforms, multi-branch retail companies, agency owners, and businesses that use tools like Tally, Zoho Books, Shopify, RazorPay, and Meta Ads. It is designed for managers who want automated executive reporting without maintaining databases internally.

How does it work?

The onboarding process follows four steps:
1. Discovery: A 20-minute call to select your key metrics.
2. Connection: We establish secure read-only access to your accounts.
3. Building: We construct your data schemas and deliver your interactive dashboard in 7 days.
4. Maintenance: We update the metrics monthly and schedule walkthrough training calls.

What makes it different?

Most analytics tools sell generic templates that break when schemas change or ledger entries mismatch. Nexiora is a managed service—meaning we handle data mapping, cleaning, server hosting, and monthly dashboard revisions. You get clean, reconciled reports without technical overhead.

Is it free to use?

Joining the waitlist is free and reserves a spot in our founding cohort. There is no cost or financial obligation. Once we begin building your data workspace, we offer structured monthly subscription plans based on your database size and connected integrations.
